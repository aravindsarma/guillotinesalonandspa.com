Rewards Program

How do I Get Started?

You are automatically signed up for our rewards program! This is a complimentary program set up as a courtesy to all of our wonderful guests. Appreciation Points are accrued through everyday transactions.

How Much are my Appreciation Points Worth?

1,000 points = $1. This is automatically credited to your account in our database and may be redeemed when you accumulate 10 thousand points which is equivalent to $10

Ways to Earn Appreciation Points

  • Receive 5,000 points when you pre-book your next appointment during your first visit.
  • Receive 10,000 points for referring another guest.
  • Receive 2,000 points when you pre-book your next appointment.
  • Receive 250 points for purchasing $50 or more in retail.
  • Receive 500 points for every $100 or more spent in services.
  • Receive 1,000 for trying a service for the first time.

Terms of Use

  • Appreciation Points are not redeemable for cash and may not be used towards gratuity.
  • Appreciation Points will only be awarded for pre-booked appointments once you have received that service. Points will not be awarded to any pre-booked appointments that are cancelled or result in a no-show.
  • Appreciation Points may be redeemed after an accumulation of 10 thousand points and may only be used in that increment.
  • Points expire December 31st. You may use any remaining points in your account up to 10,000 points before they expire on the 31st so as not to lose them. You begin earning points for the new year on January 1st.
  • Guests are responsible for claiming their points.
  • We reserve the right to make changes to our rewards system at any time.
